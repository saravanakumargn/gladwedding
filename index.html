What is GladWedding?

GladWedding is a helpful mobile app for couples and families to plan their wedding from start to finish. You can manage events, organize work, track spending, and get ideas for decoration, food, clothes, and more – all in one place.

What You Can Do with the App

📅 Plan Your Events

Keep track of all wedding functions like engagement, mehendi, reception, and more.

📝 To-Do Lists

Write down what needs to be done for each event and mark them when finished.

📞 Manage Your Vendors

Save details of photographers, caterers, decorators, and more – all in one place.

💰 Budget Tracker

Note down your spending and stay within your budget easily.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Can Join

Parents and family can also see the plan and help with updates.

🌐 Tamil & English

You can use the app in your preferred language – Tamil or English.

  • ✅ No need for paper notes or phone reminders
  • ✅ Helps avoid confusion and last-minute rush
  • ✅ Keeps everything safe and in one app
